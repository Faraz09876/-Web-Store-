Order and Delivery

This section has all information regarding ordering process and delivery details

How to process an order?

To process an order, choose a product from the website, click on add to cart, and then proceed to checkout. Fill in your details and submit the order. A confirmation will be sent and our representative will contact you within 48 hours.

What is the average delivery time?

The average delivery time is 5–7 days.

How to check the status of delivery?

You can track delivery status using the Order ID provided at the time of purchase.

What are the shipping charges?

We charge a flat 300/- PKR shipping rate per order.

Are there any places where the delivery is restricted?

Yes, deliveries to military protected areas are restricted. Please check local customs for more info.

Payment

This section has all information regarding payment methods and procedures.

How to pay cash on delivery (COD)?

Select COD as the payment method during checkout. You’ll pay upon delivery.

Are multiple orders delivered at the same time?

Yes, multiple items are combined into a single package when possible.

What are the available payment methods?

- Credit/Debit Card Online Payment
- COD (Cash on Delivery)
